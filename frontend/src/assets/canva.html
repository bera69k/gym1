Zoom workout virtual backgrounds by Canva

Come lockdowns or busy schedules, the grind doesn’t stop. So for those days when you have to take your workouts online, keep up the motivation high with cool Zoom workout backgrounds from Canva.

These Zoom backgrounds are 100% customizable to suit every kind of workout session. Whether you’re a gym instructor hosting online coaching, a fitness influencer recording a tutorial for your channel, or a regular fitness enthusiast exercising with your friends, Canva has a backdrop for you. Browse this section to find Zoom background templates to for your virtual workout sessions. You can easily tailor any design using Canva’s beginner-friendly editor.

You don’t need any art or design background to work on a design. The online editor is intuitive and straightforward, and it has all the tools and resources you need to create high-quality backgrounds, in one easy-to-navigate platform. To get started, simply find the Zoom workout background you like best, and click or tap on it to go to the online editor. There, you can make all the tweaks you want, just as you would change a fitness routine to suit your goals and needs.

Edit the placeholder content with details of your workout, like the name of your gym or your social media handles. Change the fonts and colors to ones you like better. Move things around, flip or rotate images, and make simple adjustments to the design elements. Add stock images or illustrations from thousands of royalty-free graphics in the content library. Or use your own photo files by dragging and dropping them onto the editor. Add pictures of your gym, workout studio, gym equipment, and more. Once you’re happy, download your custom workout Zoom background in high-quality, web-friendly formats. You can also share it with other Zoom attendees via social media or email.

Make sure you’re always hyped for virtual workouts with Zoom backgrounds custom-made for you, by you. Design yours today!
